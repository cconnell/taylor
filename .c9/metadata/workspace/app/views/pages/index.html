Work

\n
\n

Art

\n

After almost graduating with a degree in Theater from Anderson University I moved to Chicago to become a comedian. Unfortunately I stumbled into a career path that provides me with financial stability and comforts that my theater education taught me was unobtainable. \n

Despite that I can be spotted performing Improv every Friday at 10:00PM at The Comedy Clubhouse on Ashland and performance art / monologue based plays most months with The Drinking and Writing Theater at Haymarket Pub & Brewery.Go see more live theater. Even if it's bad.
